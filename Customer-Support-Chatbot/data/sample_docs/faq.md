# Frequently Asked Questions

## Account Management

### How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, visit the login page and click "Forgot Password".
Enter your registered email address. You will receive a reset link within 5 minutes.
The link expires after 24 hours. If you don't receive it, check your spam folder.

### How do I update my email address?
Go to Settings > Account > Email Address. Enter your new email and confirm with
your current password. A verification email will be sent to the new address.

### How do I delete my account?
Account deletion is permanent. Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account.
You will need to confirm your password. All data will be removed within 30 days.

## Billing & Payments

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and Apple Pay.
All transactions are encrypted and secure.

### How do I get a refund?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all physical products.
Digital products are non-refundable unless defective.
To request a refund, contact support@company.com with your order number.

### Why was I charged twice?
Duplicate charges are investigated within 3-5 business days.
Please provide your transaction IDs to billing@company.com for faster resolution.
Refunds for duplicate charges are processed within 7-10 business days.

## Orders & Shipping

### How do I track my order?
You will receive a tracking email when your order ships.
You can also track at yoursite.com/orders using your order number.

### What if my order arrives damaged?
Take photos of the damage and contact support within 48 hours of delivery.
We will arrange a replacement or refund at no cost to you.

### Can I cancel my order?
Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of placement.
After that, you must wait for delivery and initiate a return.

## Technical Support

### The app won't load. What should I do?
1. Clear your browser cache and cookies
2. Try a different browser or device
3. Check our status page at status.yoursite.com
4. Contact support if the issue persists

### How do I export my data?
Go to Settings > Privacy > Export Data.
Your data export will be ready within 24 hours and sent to your email.
